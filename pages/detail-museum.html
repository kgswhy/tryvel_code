Museum Di Tengah Kebun

★★★★★ 4.3 (98 reviews)
Jakarta Selatan, Indonesia

About

Museum Di Tengah Kebun is a unique private museum located in the heart of Jakarta. This hidden gem showcases an impressive collection of Indonesian and international artifacts, surrounded by lush gardens. The museum offers a peaceful retreat from the city's hustle and bustle while providing an educational experience about Indonesia's rich cultural heritage.
